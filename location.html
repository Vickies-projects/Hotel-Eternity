Our Location

We are located in the heart of the city, providing easy access to both urban attractions and serene escapes. Visit us and experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience.

Nearby Attractions:

  • City Mall - 5 minutes walk
  • Central Park - 10 minutes drive
  • International Airport - 20 minutes drive

How to Reach Us

By Car: Use GPS navigation to our address and find convenient parking on-site.

By Public Transport: The nearest subway/bus stop is a short walk from the hotel.

From the Airport: We offer shuttle services, or you can take a taxi/Uber directly to the hotel.

By Bicycle: Enjoy a scenic ride through designated bike paths leading directly to the hotel.
